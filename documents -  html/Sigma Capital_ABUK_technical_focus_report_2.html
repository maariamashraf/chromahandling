ABUK - Technical Focus Report, Wednesday 05 June 2024

Abou Kir Fertilizers ABUK 58.78 -3.02 (-4.89%)
Trend Recommendation Sell Rallies

The stock price has experienced a sharp decline, breaking below the lower boundary of the sideways trend it was following and trading below the important support level of EGP 59.50.

If this downward break is confirmed, it is recommended to activate a stop-loss order. A break below this level would signal a short-term bearish trend, paving the way for further declines with an initial target around the 48.00 EGP support area.

This bearish outlook will remain in place as long as the stock trades below the first resistance level, which now represents a potential pivot point for a reversal if it is broken upwards.
